PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

HARD CONSTRAINTS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- No middleware platform: NO ESB, NO iPaaS, NO message broker (Kafka/Rabbit/EventHub/etc.), NO workflow engine.
- Direct connections only:
  A) Batch/static/bulk via Staging DB (preferred) and/or flat files (fallback)
  B) Transactional/near-real-time via direct REST to DDM and from DDM to targets
- DDM is the ONLY transformation layer. DDM may persist messages (inbox/outbox/error) and provide replay tooling, but must NOT become an orchestration engine.
- Explicit System-of-Record ownership per domain and strict governance for routing/BOM versions.
